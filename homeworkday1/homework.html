J & R Shared Refab

J & R Shared Refab provides a full blown workshop for people to build on a hobby, create a living for themselves, or just enjoy the freedom of having alomst any fabrication tool and machine at their disposal

We Specialize in creating refabricated works through our sourcing and use of reclaimed materials

We provide :

  • Woodworking, metal fabrication, painting tools, and much more
  • A comfortable, open, inspiring work environment
  • Abundant resources of reclaimed material for use in your projects
  • A place for you to let your creative side run free

What do we offer that other coworking shops dont?

J & R isn't just a coworking shop. We are a ctreative space that caters to those wanting to find ways of expressing themselves in a very hands on sort of way. We love to share our knowledge, and encourage our members to extend out to one another and collaborate on projects and learn from eachother. We have a stage and love to jam out while working, and are happy to stop for a jam sesh in the middle of grinding burred edges or prepping a project for paint. We are in the business of connecting people, forming ideas, and creating works of art in the form you find natural.

Sound pretty cool? We think so, and we'd love to show you around the shop and get you started on your next project.
